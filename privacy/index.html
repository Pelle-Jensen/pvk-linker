Privacy policy

PVK Premium (the “App”) is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose personal information from users of the App.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information from you, such as your name, email address, phone number, or location, when you use the App. We may also collect non-personal information, such as your device type or IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information to:

  • Provide you with the services and features of the App
  • Respond to your requests and inquiries
  • Personalize your experience with the App
  • Analyze how users use the App and to improve the App
  • Send you promotional or marketing communications

We may share your information with third-party service providers that perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, payment processing, and customer support. We may also share your information if we are required to do so by law or if we believe that such action is necessary to comply with the law or legal process, protect our rights or property, or prevent fraud or abuse of the App.

3. Your Choices

You can always choose not to provide personal information to us. However, if you choose not to provide certain information, you may not be able to use all the features of the App. You can also opt-out of receiving promotional or marketing communications from us by following the instructions in the communications or by contacting us at pelle@gentsgroup.no.

4. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, no security system is perfect and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at pelle@gentsgroup.no to have the information removed.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you by posting a notice on the App or by sending you an email.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at pelle@gentsgroup.no
